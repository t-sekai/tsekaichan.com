SoCal ICPC 2023

Four days ago, I participated in the ICPC Southern California Regionals at Riverside City College. ICPC stands for International Collegiate Programming Contest. It was my first time participating adn representing UCSD, so I was very excited. I was part of the second UCSD team: UCSD Team 2. My teammates were Shubhum and Victor.

Since network is prohibbited during the contest, I had to compute a reference sheet the night beforehand. I spent the whole night with the help of ChatGPT to compute my 24 page reference paper for Java (which Shubhum and I uses). Here.

In the morning, I had to leave at 7:00 am to catch the Uber with my teams at 7:30. It was a raining and cold day. I held my ubrella tightly to avoid myself from the rain.
